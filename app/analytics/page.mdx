# Analytics

exit1.dev provides comprehensive analytics to help you understand the performance and availability of your monitored services.

## Features

### Logs
Detailed check execution history powered by BigQuery. Filter by date, status, and search for specific events. Add notes and annotations to log entries.

[View Logs documentation](/analytics/logs)

### Reports
SLA compliance metrics, response time distribution analysis, and incident interval reporting.

[View Reports documentation](/analytics/reports)

### Uptime Statistics
Detailed uptime calculations with performance breakdowns including DNS, connect, TLS, and TTFB timing.

[View Uptime Statistics documentation](/analytics/uptime-statistics)

# Integrations

Connect exit1.dev with your existing tools and workflows.

## Available Integrations

### Webhooks
Send check events to any HTTP endpoint. Use webhooks to integrate with incident management tools, custom dashboards, or internal systems.

[Configure Webhooks](/integrations/webhooks)

### Slack
Receive monitoring alerts directly in your Slack channels. Get notified when checks go down or recover.

[Set up Slack](/integrations/slack)

### Discord
Receive monitoring alerts in your Discord server. Get notified in the channels where your team collaborates.

[Set up Discord](/integrations/discord)

## Integration Comparison

| Feature | Webhooks | Slack | Discord |
|---|---|---|---|
| Real-time alerts | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Custom payload | Yes | No | No |
| Retry on failure | Yes (8 attempts) | Yes | Yes |
| Health monitoring | Yes | No | No |
| Max per account | 5 | 1 | 1 |

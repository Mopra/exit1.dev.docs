# Creating Your Account

## Sign Up

Visit [app.exit1.dev](https://app.exit1.dev) to create your account. exit1.dev uses **Clerk** for authentication, giving you multiple sign-in options:

- **Email and password** - Traditional email-based registration
- **Google** - Sign in with your Google account
- **GitHub** - Sign in with your GitHub account

## Account Setup

After signing up, you'll be taken directly to your dashboard. No email verification is required to start monitoring.

## Your Profile

You can manage your profile settings from the dashboard:

- **Display name** - How you appear in the app
- **Email address** - Used for account notifications and email alerts
- **Connected accounts** - Manage your SSO connections

## Security

exit1.dev leverages Clerk's security infrastructure, which includes:

- Secure session management
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) support
- Automatic session expiry

## Next Steps

Once your account is created, proceed to [Your First Check](/getting-started/your-first-check) to start monitoring.

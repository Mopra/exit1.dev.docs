# Getting Started

Welcome to exit1.dev! This guide will walk you through creating your account, setting up your first monitoring check, and navigating the dashboard.

## What is exit1.dev?

exit1.dev is a website monitoring platform that tracks the availability, performance, and health of your websites, APIs, and network endpoints. With exit1.dev, you can:

- **Monitor uptime** of websites, REST APIs, and TCP/UDP endpoints
- **Track SSL certificates** and domain expiration dates
- **Receive alerts** via email, SMS, webhooks, Slack, and Discord
- **Create status pages** to communicate availability to your users
- **Analyze performance** with detailed logs, reports, and uptime statistics
- **Access data programmatically** through the REST API

## Quick Start

1. [Create your account](/getting-started/creating-your-account) using email or social sign-in
2. [Add your first check](/getting-started/your-first-check) to start monitoring
3. [Explore the dashboard](/getting-started/understanding-the-dashboard) to view results

## Free Plan

The Free plan is ideal for individuals and small projects that need basic monitoring:

- Monitor up to 50 endpoints
- 5-minute check intervals
- Email alerting (10/hour, 10/month)
- 1 webhook integration
- 1 public status page
- SSL certificate monitoring
- API access
- Analytics & logs (30 days)
- Bulk import and bulk edit

## Nano Plan

The Nano plan is designed for professionals and growing teams:

- Unlimited monitors
- 1-minute check intervals
- Multi-region checks
- SMS alerts and team alerts
- Higher alert budgets (1,000 emails and 20 SMS per month)

## Upgrading

To upgrade from Free to Nano:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade to Nano**
3. Enter your payment information
4. Your account is upgraded immediately

## Downgrading

To downgrade from Nano to Free:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan** > **Free**
3. Your plan changes at the end of the current billing period
4. Checks and status pages exceeding Free plan limits will be paused

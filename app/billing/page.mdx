## Free

Good for individuals and small side projects.

- 10 monitors, 5-minute intervals
- Email alerts (10/hr, 10/mo)
- 1 webhook integration
- 1 public status page
- SSL certificate monitoring
- 60 days of history
- Bulk import and bulk edit

## Nano — $9/mo

For hobbyists and small sites that need faster checks and a proper status page.

- 50 monitors, 2-minute intervals
- Email alerts (50/hr, 1,000/mo)
- 5 webhook integrations
- 5 status pages with the drag-and-drop builder
- Domain Intelligence (WHOIS, expiry, DNS)
- Maintenance mode
- 60 days of history

## Pro — $24/mo

## Agency — $49/mo

## Upgrading

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan** and pick a tier
3. Enter your payment information
4. Your account is upgraded immediately

## Downgrading

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan** and pick a lower tier
3. Your plan changes at the end of the current billing period
4. Resources above the new tier's limits are paused: checks beyond the monitor cap, extra webhooks, extra status pages, and — when leaving Pro or Agency — API keys and SMS alerts
